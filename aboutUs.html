About Us
What is Midnight Robotics
Started in fall of 2021 by five MSOE students with the goal of creating an unparalleled combat robotics collegiate organization, by providing student the opportunity to grow their competencies to be ever more prepared for their professional career after college through (collaborative) workshops, group projects and informative talks.
Guiding Principles
Guiding principles serve to guide thinking- mainly decision making- within an organization by utilizing/incorporating the beliefs and values of the organization
  1. Advocate for yourself. No one else will
  2. Strive for excellence, always.
  3. Go get it
  4. Look at the tree and the forest
  5. Build Relationships
  6. Be net positive
  7. Great minds don't think alike
  8. Celebrate our victories
